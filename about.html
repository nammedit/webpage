Martin Tidemann Kvalø is a composer working in the intersection between instrumental and algorithmic composition. He is currently doing his master’s in composition at the Norwegian Academy of Music with a focus on computer-aided composition. In his master’s project, Kvalø works at reconciling the computer’s power of strict logical thinking with intuitive "hands-on" musicality.

Martin is also a performer of electronics in the new-music-trio Mujic Footure together with countertenor Sean Bell and pianist Eirik Hellerdal Fosstveit. They recently played at the UNM festival (Ung Nordisk Musikk) (You can listen to the performance here).
