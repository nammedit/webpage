Martin Tidemann Kvalø (b. 1994) is a Norwegian composer working mainly with instrumentations mixing electronic and acoustical instruments and sounds. Using self-authored algorithmic tools in the programming language Supercollider together with improvisation and classical compositional techniques Kvalø searches for the other-worldly, the transcendent, the alien and at the same time strangely familiar and recognizable in his music.

In one of his more recent works "Dada-rekviem" for the new-music-trio "Mujic Footure", he explores synthesized versions between the Latin requiem-texts and James Joyce' "Finnegans wake" in a sound world built on contrapunctal textures and chaos-like algorithms controlling the synthesizer.

Kvalø's music has recently been played around the west-coast of Norway, on the "Vegadaga" festival at the world-heritage-island Vega and at the classical music festival "Norsjø Kammermusikkfestival" in Telemark Norway.
